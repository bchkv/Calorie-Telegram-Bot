Studies show typical self-report error of 20–50% or more, even with apps like MyFitnessPal. — https://openreview.net/pdf?id=XKgAsQxSU5

This app aims to provide a low friction way to log your food intake.

Benchmarks have been run thoroughly to ensure the app produces appropriate numbers. Big reputable scientific datasets are used. 
They include hundreds of dishes photos and precise macronutrient estimations. This app was tested against this
groundtruth data — https://skynet.ecn.purdue.edu/~coburn6/ACETADA/
